
# MyBaby Product Knowledge Base

## MyBaby SaaS - Nursery Management System

### Core Features:
- **Attendance Tracking**: Real-time check-in/out with biometric or QR codes
- **Parent Communication**: In-app messaging, notifications, daily reports
- **Financial Management**: Invoicing, payment tracking, expense management
- **Staff Management**: Scheduling, payroll, performance tracking
- **Meal Planning**: Dietary requirements, menu planning
- **Health Records**: Medical history, vaccination tracking, incident reports
- **Analytics & Reports**: Attendance reports, financial dashboards, growth metrics

### Pricing:
- Starter: SAR 500/month (up to 50 children)
- Professional: SAR 1,200/month (up to 150 children)
- Enterprise: Custom pricing (150+ children)

### Target Audience:
Nursery owners, administrators, and staff in Saudi Arabia

## MyBaby مُـلاك - Parent Mobile App

### Core Features:
- **Find Nurseries**: Search by location, ratings, price, facilities
- **Real-time Availability**: See available slots instantly
- **Online Booking**: Reserve spots with secure payment
- **Provider Profiles**: Ratings, reviews, photos, certifications
- **Notifications**: Updates on child's activities, meals, naps
- **Emergency Backup Care**: Quick access to backup providers

### Benefits:
- Save time finding quality childcare
- Transparent pricing and reviews
- Secure online payments
- Peace of mind with verified providers

### Free to Download:
Available on iOS and Android

## MyBaby Marketplace - Provider Network

### For Parents:
- Browse 500+ verified providers across Saudi Arabia
- Filter by location, price, age group, facilities
- Read authentic reviews from other parents
- Book tours and reserve spots online
- Access emergency backup care network

### For Providers:
- Increase visibility to thousands of parents
- Manage bookings and payments digitally
- Build reputation through verified reviews
- Access business analytics and insights
- Professional listing with photos and details

### Commission:
- 10% transaction fee on bookings
- Free listing for first 3 months
- Premium listing: SAR 300/month
